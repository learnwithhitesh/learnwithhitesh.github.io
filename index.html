DSA Course

Patterns-first DSA for 0–5 YOE. We start from basics and take you to expert level.

  • Foundations: arrays, strings, hashing, two-pointers, sliding window
  • Trees/Graphs: traversal, shortest paths, union-find, topological sort
  • DP: knapsack, subsequences, matrix DP, state modeling
  • Greedy, heaps, sorting, binary search on answers
  • Communication: complexity, trade-offs, explaining approaches clearly
  • Mock interviews focused on DSA with actionable feedback
  • Weekly assignments + review to track progress

Why DSA matters

DSA sharpens problem solving and communication under constraints. We start from basics and take you to expert level with patterns, complexity trade-offs, and interview-ready explanations.

System Design Course

LLD + HLD from fundamentals to scale. Build production-grade design skills.

  • LLD: clean APIs, modular design, patterns, testing
  • HLD: data modeling, sharding, caching, queues, storage
  • Consistency models, idempotency, and reliability
  • Observability: logging, metrics, tracing basics
  • Trade-offs, capacity estimates, and scaling strategies
  • Case studies with whiteboarding and critiques
  • Mock system design interviews with feedback on structure and trade-offs
